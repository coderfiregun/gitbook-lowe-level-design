### Introduction

In software engineering, the ability to design robust and efficient systems is paramount. **Low-Level Design (LLD)** focuses on the internal working of individual components and how they interact within a system. As companies increasingly prioritize practical design and problem-solving skills, mastering LLD is essential for aspiring and growing software engineers.

This crash course equips you with foundational knowledge and practical skills to tackle real-world design challenges confidently, covering key concepts, essential design patterns, and frequently asked LLD problems.
